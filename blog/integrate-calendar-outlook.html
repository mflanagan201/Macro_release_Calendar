Integrating the Economic Calendar in Outlook

In just a few simple steps you can have instant access to the economic calendar within Microsoft Outlook. This is a great way to ensure you never miss an important economic release.

  1. Copy this calendar link:
    https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/9b1e48819517c85b915b3922e3c1b144%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics
  2. In Outlook, go to Home > Add Calendar > From Internet.
  3. Paste the URL and confirm subscription.
