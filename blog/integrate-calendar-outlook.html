Integrating Internet Calendars with Outlook

Subscribing to internet calendars in Outlook is a great way to stay on top of important economic releases.

  1. Copy the calendar link (usually ends in .ics).
  2. In Outlook, go to Home > Add Calendar > From Internet.
  3. Paste the URL and confirm subscription.
